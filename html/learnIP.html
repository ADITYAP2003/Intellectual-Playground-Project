Learn Intellectual Property Rights

Copyright

Copyright: Saying "This is mine," controlling use, deciding who can use it.

Trademark

Trademarks: Special stamp showing "This is from us," identifying products..

Patent

Patents: Special shield for inventors, only they can make/sell inventions.

Trade Secretes

Trade secrets: Hidden treasures for businesses, like secret recipes or methods.

Industrial design rights

Industrial design: Protecting unique looks from copying without permission.

Plant variety rights

Plant variety rights: Only seller of new plant types.

Geographical indications

Geographical indications: Tying products to specific places' special qualities.

Semiconductor chip

Semiconductor chip protection: Guarding intricate chip designs from copying.

Database rights

Database rights: Securing databases from unauthorized use or copying.

Utility Models

Utility models: Like patents for new product improvements.
